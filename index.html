About

Welcome! I'm Natasha Clark and I'm an aspiring Full Stack web developer (with a particular love for front-end tech and user experience) in the Washington D.C area. After nearly 5 years in sales and account management, multiple forays in and out of the tech world, and more than my fair share of “is it too late to do what I love?” moments, I decided it was time for a change. In January of 2017, I started my journey into the world of web development and I'm look forward to the adventures my new field has in store.


Here is what my resume won't tell you

I am an optimist, an architect, and dedicated problem-solver. My friends can always depend on me to make the call on where to eat and my employers can always depend on me to drive their teams to program goals.

I love to travel because I believe understanding the world around us better equips us to improve it in meaningful ways. I was raised to leave things better than how you found them.

I strive to be the change I want to see in the world. It's important to me that more women get involved in the tech industry and part of my long-term professional goals is to inspire other women to enter the field as well.

I believe passion and curiosity fuel the best solutions, as well as the brightest minds, and I look forward to working with groups who share this view.
